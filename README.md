A 2D platformer and parkour game developed for **Z-Jam 3** under the theme "The Last Day."

## Premise
The Solar Defense Has Fallen. The world depends on an artificial light that keeps "The Dark Ones" at bay. The systems have failed, and the final sunset is approaching.

You are **Runner 7**. Your mission is to carry the Reset Key from the city ruins to the Transmission Tower before the sun sets completely.

## Tech Stack
* **Engine:** Godot Engine 4.5.
* **Language:** GDScript.
* **Project Status:** Prototype / Game Jam Version.
